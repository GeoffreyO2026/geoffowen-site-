YOUTUBE CHANNEL DESCRIPTION — BIOLOGICALLY ALIGNED PARENTING

Biologically Aligned Parenting is a framework created by Geoff Owen over forty years of research, observation, and lived experience as a father.

Its central premise is simple. Behaviour is not the problem. Behaviour is the signal.

Children are born with innate biological expectations shaped over hundreds of thousands of years. When those expectations are met, children are calm, cooperative, and easy to be with. When they are not met, they signal their distress through behaviour. All behaviour.

This channel is for high performing fathers who feel they are doing everything right and still something at home is not working. For parents living with a child whose behaviour resists every strategy they bring to it. And for anyone who suspects that the framework modern parenting has given them may not be the right one.

It is not about the time. It was never about the time.

Geoffrey Owen is the author of ODD: A Non-Clinical Approach to Children Who Say No, and The Parenting Assumption.
