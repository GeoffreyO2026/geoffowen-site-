Working with Me

This work begins with
a conversation,
not a programme.

Most fathers arrive with a situation that is not shifting and a feeling that nothing they have tried is working. From there we look at what is driving it, understand what the child's biology is actually expecting, and begin to replace what has been missing. There are four ways we can work together, depending on where you are and what you need.

When something needs to shift, before anything else
You're not a bad dad.
Take Back Your Sanity

Some evenings you arrive with little left to give, and it does not take long before something in you reacts in a way you would rather it had not. You know it is not who you are, and the not knowing why it keeps happening can weigh almost as much as the moment itself. This is not the start of a programme. It is a single, focused session to look at your child's behaviour through a different lens, and your own, so what has actually been going on becomes clear.

This is not a diagnosis of your child, and it is not a fix for everything. It is a conversation to discover what is actually driving your reactions toward your child, and how to bring greater calm when there feels like there is little left to give.

How every programme begins

Before We Begin

Every engagement, regardless of which programme you choose, begins with a dedicated session before any formal work starts. This is not an intake form or an administrative step. It is the first piece of real work. We go deep into your specific situation, your child, your home dynamic, and what you are hoping will change. From there we agree together on a precise and observable outcome. This session sets the foundation and the direction for everything that follows.

Tier One
The Foundation Programme
£500 per month  ·  minimum three months

This is where most fathers begin. You come with a situation that is not shifting and a feeling that nothing you have tried is working. Over three months of fortnightly conversations I teach you the BAP framework in full. You learn to read what your child's behaviour is actually communicating, understand what their biology is expecting from you, and begin to replace what has been missing.

There is no outcome guarantee at this level, but a full satisfaction promise. If at any point you feel the work is not delivering value, you can stop. Credit applies toward Tier Two if you choose to go further, paying only the difference.

Tier Three
Just for Parents
£6,000 three months  ·  both partners

For parents who recognise that the most effective change happens when both partners are working from the same framework. This programme brings both partners into the work together. It is not relationship counselling and it is not couples therapy. It is parenting alignment.

Both parents learn to read the same signals, understand the same biology, and respond from the same place. When two parents are moving in the same direction, the change at home is faster and deeper. Tier Four
The Family Legacy Advisory
£25,000 first year  ·  whole family

For founders and executives who have recognised that their professional success and their family life are pulling in opposite directions, and that something has to change. This is a private mentorship relationship. We work together weekly to begin, moving to fortnightly as stability is established.

The work goes beyond any single situation. It addresses the deeper patterns that are shaping how you show up at home, how your child experiences you, and what kind of relationship you are building for the long term. Outcome guaranteed. Most relationships continue well beyond the first year because the work earns its place.
